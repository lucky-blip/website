Getting Started



Create MetaMask Wallet

Download and install the MetaMask extension for your browser and follow the setup process to create a new wallet.

Select the Polygon Network

Go to the MetaMask network selection dropdown and choose "Add Network," then enter Polygon's network details to connect.

Send WMATIC to your wallet

Copy your MetaMask wallet address, go to an exchange where you have WMATIC, and send it to the copied address.


Swap WMATIC for TERRYCOIN

Access to Uniswap via the link below in the page, connect your MetaMask, then swap your WMATIC for TERRYCOIN.
