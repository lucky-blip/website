TERRYCOIN

Inspired by the internet meme "Terry the Fat Shark"




Who is Terry the Shark ?

Terry the Shark is known for his kindness and he has a unique tradition: every Wednesday, he presents a "Token of Friendship" to a fellow sea creature.

This act of kindness inspired the creation of "TerryCoin," a cryptocurrency named after him, symbolizing the value of giving and community in the digital world.
